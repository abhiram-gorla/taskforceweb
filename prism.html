PRISM


The ‘PRogram to Initiate Student Mentoring’ aims to acquaint freshers with the different aspects of college life at NITK Surathkal. From clubs to sports, academics to fests, PRISM provides a platform for the newbies to explore the different opportunities provided by the college and make the most out of it.

PRISM also sets up Helpdesk for the freshers during admission. Helpdesk is an initiative to aid the freshers resolve any queries through one-on-one interactions during the course of admissions.

Please visit the website PRISM for more information.
