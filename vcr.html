VCR

Virtual ClassRoom is a project to aid the students of the first year in their academics. It seeks to promote learning through an interactive audio-visual medium and help students have a better understanding of concepts and theories. The following are the undertakings by VCR:
  • Providing videos of lectures taken up by the seniors on various topics in their curriculum.
  • Videos introducing the freshmen to all the courses which will include -
    • A brief understanding of all the topics that will be covered in the course throughout the semester.
    • Mandatory and preferable textbooks and other reference materials.
  • Conducting Knowledge Exchange Programmes [KEP sessions] by the technical clubs in college.
